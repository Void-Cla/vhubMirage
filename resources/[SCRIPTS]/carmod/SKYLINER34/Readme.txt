Description :
This mod is made for YCA-y97y's Add-on Nissan Skyline GT-R (BNR34) : https://www.gta5-mods.com/vehicles/nissan-skyline-gt-r-bnr34-yca-y97y
But you can use it on any car by changing <audioNameHash /> to <audioNameHash>ELEGY2</audioNameHash> in the appropriate vehicle.meta.
For example, you can use it on MrEverest's 1999 Nissan Skyline GT-R (R34) : https://www.gta5-mods.com/vehicles/1999-nissan-skyline-gt-r-r34
For MrEverest's car, the vehicle.meta is in Grand Theft Auto V\(mods)\update\update.rpf\common\data, as for most cars.
Because this mod replaces the Elegy's engine sound, some other cars using the same engine sound will sound similar. 
It also includes the original files.
I hope you'll enjoy it !

Installation :
- Extract "GTA V Nissan Skyline R34 Engine Sound by Hippy.zip" anywhere you want.
- In the mod's files, choose the version of the sound you want to install ("Version 1" or "Version 2") by listening at the samples in each versions.
- Go in the folder of the version you chose
- Replace supercar_5_jp_v6.awc in Grand Theft Auto V\(mods)\x64\audio\sfx\STREAMED_VEHICLES_GRANULAR.rpf\ 
- Replace supercar_5_jp_v6_npc.awc in Grand Theft Auto V\mods\x64\audio\sfx\STREAMED_VEHICLES_GRANULAR_NPC.rpf\
- The mod is installed ! You can launch your game, and enjoy your new Nissan Skyline GT-R R34 RB26DETT sound !
